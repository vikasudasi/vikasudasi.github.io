Built as CTO of Saathi, BhAI is an AI-powered assistant that revolutionizes how India's blue- and grey-collar workforce discovers and secures jobs. It combines cutting-edge AI with practical, accessible interfaces.

**Tech Stack:**
- **Conversational AI:** GPT-4o for natural language understanding and response generation
- **Voice Intelligence:** LiveKit + Sarvam AI for real-time multilingual TTS/STT (Hindi & English)
- **Vector Search:** MongoDB Atlas Vector Search for semantic job matching
- **Orchestration:** LangChain for complex AI workflow coordination
- **Product Features:** Job reels engine, referral wallet systems, AI interview screening flows
